About Campus Sakshar

Campus Sakshar is a platform dedicated to empowering B.Tech students by providing a collaborative environment for learning and sharing knowledge. Our website enables students from various colleges to access notes, share their own study material, and connect with peers to exchange skills.

Our main features include:

  • 📤 Upload Notes: Share your personal notes to help fellow students.
  • 📚 View Notes: Access notes from different colleges and subjects easily.
  • 🤝 Peer-to-Peer Exchange: Connect with students to exchange skills and collaborate on projects.
  • 🌐 User-Friendly Interface: Navigate easily with search and responsive design.
  • 🌙 Dark Mode Support: Reduce eye strain with a toggleable dark mode.

Our mission is to create a community where students help each other succeed academically while learning new skills collaboratively.
